 Got Wood Floors

About us

Got Wood Floors is a family-owned business dedicated to bringing high quality wood to your homes. Whether you need floor installation or repairs, we are ready to help you with 25 years of experience under our belt. Why choose us? Many floor companies nowadays use cheap underlying fiberboard for their wood floors which warp and break down faster and easier over time. Got Wood Floors only use REAL wood, making sure you get what you paid for. If you are looking for high quality wood and good service at an affordable price or have any questions, contact us now.

Services

Refinish

Need to apply a new finish to your hardwood floors? We got you covered. Whether your floors have a faded finish or light scatches, we will be able to refinish your hardwood floors. Don't know if you should refinsh your floors? Click here to find out.

Installation

Installing new floors are a daunting and time-consuming task. As professionals, we make sure that we provide realiable and high quality service when installing floors and finish in a timely-manner. We will be able to install all types of hardwood floors such as engineered, glued down, and floating floors.

Repairs

Are your floors damaged? In need of repairs? We'll fix your floors. We take care of problems like sagging wood panels, rotted floor josts, squeaky floors, buckling /crowning, fading/discoloring, and warping.

Patches

Have a stain you can't get out on your wood floors? Were your walls or cabinets moved? Your old vents or duct openings obsolete now? We offer patching services for these types of situations. We will replace your missing subfloor, design your patch, remove unwanted wood and fill the empty space with hardwood.

Recoats

Want to restore the glow on your floors and give them a refreshed look? We offer a screen and recoat service, also know as a buff and coat. We spruce up an existing coat of polyurethane by top-coating it to make your floors look as good as new.

Distressed Floors

Ever wanted the look and charm of a vintage wood floor? We can distress your floors to make it so. The concept of creating a distressed look is easy, but it is ltime-comnsuming, labor-intensive and requires some artistic skills. Leave it to us to give your floors that lived-in and vintage feel.
